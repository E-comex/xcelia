Integrated Higher Secondary

Course Overview

Integrated Higher Secondary education refers to a comprehensive educational program that combines academic subjects with skill-based training, vocational courses, or entrance exam preparation. This approach aims to provide students with a holistic education that prepares them for higher studies, careers, and life.

Available Streams

COMMERCE

Students can complete their Higher Secondary with:

  • CA Foundation
  • CMA Foundation
  • ACCA Knowledge level

HUMANITIES

Students have ability to face competitive exams:

  • CUET
  • NCET
  • CLAT
